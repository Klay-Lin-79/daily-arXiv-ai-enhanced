### [1] [Self-Referential $K$-SAT and the Finite Analogue of Gödel's Incompleteness Theorem](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.01671)
*Wen Fang,Xianxian Li,Jun Liu,Jie Luo,Yongxin Tong,Ke Xu*

Main category: cs.CC

TL;DR: 本文通过在布尔K-SAT中建立哥德尔不完备定理的有限组合类比，证明自指性和解独立性导致的不可区分性使得任何亚线性窗口的演绎管道都存在信息盲区，迫使Resolation反驳使用宽子句并引发指数级证明树爆炸，进而将SETH重新解释为哥德尔不完备性在有限计算上的投影。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 自指性和解独立性是计算难解性的核心属性，但标准随机K-SAT中的赋值相关性破坏了独立性。作者希望建立一个有限组合系统中类似于哥德尔不完备定理的结构，以理解计算复杂性的根本来源。

Method: 使用对数宽度集成(K=O(log N))，使可满足赋值收敛到泊松分布；通过基于唯一解的单子句替换，构造结构上不可约简的SAT/UNSAT对。利用算法信息论和香农信道证明亚线性窗口的信息盲区，分析Resolation证明的宽子句需求和证明树复杂度。

Result: 证明任何亚线性窗口的演绎管道都要面对信息盲区，导致描述下界K(A) ≥ Ω(N^{1-δ})；UNSAT实例的Resolation反驳必须使用宽子句(w(π) ≥ Ω(N^{1-δ}))，引发指数级证明树爆炸(S(φ) ≥ exp(Ω(N^{1-2δ})))；当δ→0+时收敛到最坏情况2^N阈值。

Conclusion: 将SETH重新解释为哥德尔不完备性在有限计算上的直接投影；诊断了复杂性理论长期停滞的原因；指出这种自指性硬度具有物理不变性——量子计算无法规避（需要全局语义分析），机器学习架构在损耗性局部压缩上存在扩展瓶颈。

Abstract: Self-reference and solution independence are core properties underlying intractability. This paper establishes a finite combinatorial analogue of Gödel's incompleteness theorems within Boolean $K$-SAT. While standard random $K$-SAT has assignment correlations that disrupt solution independence, we resolve this via a logarithmic-width ensemble ($K = O(\log N)$). Here, satisfying assignments converge to a Poisson distribution, letting unsatisfiable and uniquely satisfiable formulas coexist. By executing a single-clause substitution conditioned on the unique solution, we construct structurally irreducible SAT/UNSAT pairs that are indistinguishable via local evaluation.
  Using algorithmic information theory and Shannon channels, we prove that deductive pipelines restricted to a sublinear window suffer from an informational blind spot, forcing a descriptive lower bound of $K(\mathcal{A}) \geq Ω(N^{1-δ})$. This deficit forces any Resolution refutation of the UNSAT instance to utilize wide clauses ($w(π) \geq Ω(N^{1-δ})$), triggering an exponential proof-tree explosion ($S(φ) \geq \exp(Ω(N^{1-2δ}))$). As $δ\rightarrow 0^+$, this bound converges to the worst-case $2^N$ threshold, reframing the Strong Exponential Time Hypothesis (SETH) as a direct projection of Gödel incompleteness onto finite computation.
  We diagnose the decades-long stagnation in complexity theory. Transitioning from Turing's class separation to a Gödelian paradigm of instance indistinguishability, we introduce a multi-dimensional comparative framework that contrasts these two historical lineages across distinct perspectives. The self-referential hardness exhibits physical invariance: it precludes quantum shortcuts due to the necessity of global semantic analysis and delineates a scaling bottleneck for machine learning architectures operating on lossy, local compression.

### [2] [Partition Rank and Algebraic Circuit Lower Bounds](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.02241)
*Cornelius Brand,Petteri Kaski,Jiaheng Wang*

Main category: cs.CC

TL;DR: 本文通过引入Naslund的划分秩（partition ranks）框架，将张量秩的概念推广到高阶多重线性情形，建立了与乘法复杂度的联系，并在双线性情形下恢复了Strassen的经典结论。此外，还展示了与张量切片秩及其对称变体的联系，并给出了对称切片秩计算问题的简单NP-hard证明。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Strassen的双线性复杂度理论在更高阶多重线性情形下失效，亟需一种能够统一刻画任意常数阶多重线性情形下算术复杂度的秩理论。

Method: 利用Naslund提出的划分秩（partition ranks）框架，将广义张量秩与乘法复杂度建立联系；同时建立与Tao-Sawin切片秩及对称切片秩的关联，并给出对称切片秩NP-hard性的简洁证明。

Result: 划分秩能够在任意常数阶多重线性情形下从下界控制乘法复杂度（从而控制算术复杂度），在双线性情形下恢复Strassen的经典刻画；对称切片秩的计算问题是NP-hard的。

Conclusion: 划分秩为基于秩的方法在细粒度算法与复杂度问题（如超团猜想）中的新应用提供了可能，并为高阶多重线性复杂度的研究开辟了新路径。

Abstract: Strassen's theory of bilinear complexity provides a mathematical characterization of the arithmetic complexity of primitives such as matrix multiplication via the rank of tensors. However, the connection to tensor rank is known to break down in higher degrees of multilinearity.
  In this work, we highlight an unexplored connection between a generalized notion of tensor rank, which can be defined in Naslund's framework of partition ranks (JCTA 2020), and multiplicative complexity. These partition ranks allow us to control the multiplicative complexity, and thus arithmetic complexity, in any constant degree of multilinearity from below, while recovering Strassen's seminal characterization in the bilinear case. This enables novel potential applications of the rank-based approaches to problems in fine-grained algorithms and complexity, such as the hyperclique conjecture of Lincoln-Williams-Vassilevska Williams (SODA 2018). Moreover, we exhibit connections to established notions of rank, such as tensor slice rank (in the sense of Tao and Sawin), as well as its symmetric variant. For computing the latter symmetric variant, we point out a simple NP-hardness proof, contrasting the rather involved NP-hardness proof for ordinary, non-symmetric tensor slice rank by Bläser et al. (SODA 2021).

### [3] [CoPersona: Collaborative Persona Graphs for Robust LLM Personalization](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.01485)
*Yangtian Zhang,Leyao Wang,Hiren Madhu,Ngoc Bui,Walter Roznyatovskiy,Rex Ying*

Main category: cs.IR

TL;DR: CoPersona提出了一种基于图的协作个性化框架，通过从行为相似的同伴中借用电信号，来补全稀疏和有偏的用户历史记录，从而实现对弱观察用户属性的鲁棒LLM个性化。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现实LLM个性化面临用户历史稀疏、有偏的问题，导致弱观察的用户属性难以推断，测试时请求转向未充分支持的面相时个性化效果脆弱。

Method: 将用户交互历史分解为多面相表示，构建多重人格图建模同伴间的面相级对齐；采用双分支架构（非参数同伴检索+参数化图推理）在推理时利用同伴信息。

Result: 在多个领域和模型规模下，CoPersona相比强基线方法取得了一致的性能提升。

Conclusion: CoPersona是一种有效的鲁棒LLM个性化方法，通过图协作框架补偿稀疏用户画像。

Abstract: Real-world LLM personalization is often constrained by sparse and skewed user histories: most users provide only a handful of interactions, while even frequent users' logs capture an incomplete and biased view of their preferences. As a result, weakly observed user attributes are difficult to infer, leading to brittle personalization when test-time requests shift toward under-supported facets. Motivated by this limitation, we present CoPersona, a graph-based collaborative personalization framework that completes sparse user profiles by borrowing signals from behaviorally similar peers. However, directly transferring signals is difficult because uneven facet coverage introduces bias into interaction histories, obscuring user similarity in the unstructured global space. To address this issue, CoPersona decomposes interaction histories into multiple facet-level representations and explicitly models peer-to-peer, facet-level alignment through a multiplex persona graph. To effectively leverage peer information at inference time, we employ a dual-branch architecture that combines non-parametric peer retrieval with parametric graph reasoning. Experiments across multiple domains and model scales demonstrate consistent improvements over strong baselines, validating CoPersona as an effective approach for robust LLM personalization.

</details>
